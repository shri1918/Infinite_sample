Loan Against Property

Unlock the hidden value of your residential or commercial property. High loan amounts at lower interest rates for any personal or business needs.

Why opt for LAP?

A Loan Against Property (LAP) is a secured loan where you pledge your property as collateral. It offers significantly lower interest rates compared to personal or business loans, higher loan amounts, and longer repayment tenures (up to 15-20 years). Your property remains in your possession and you can continue using it.
